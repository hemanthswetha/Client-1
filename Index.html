About Raja Lodge, Ooty

Established in the heart of Ooty over 45 years ago, Raja Lodge is one of the oldest and most trusted stays in the Queen of Hills.

Our lodge reflects a blend of traditional hospitality and comfort with modern simplicity.

With our long-standing reputation, central location, and personal service, Raja Lodge continues to be the preferred choice.
